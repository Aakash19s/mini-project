About Us

Mind play is an interactive text-based game,it's not a traditional video game in the sense of having graphics or complex gameplay mechanics. Instead, it's more of an interactive narrative that aims to simulate the experience of living with depression.The game was designed to raise awareness about depression and to help players understand the challenges faced by people who suffer from it.

In mind play, players make choices that reflect the character's struggle with depression, and these choices can lead to different outcomes

It provides insights into how depression can impact relationships, work, and everyday life.

Some people found it to be a valuable tool for increasing empathy and understanding of depression, while others had concerns about its portrayal of mental health issues.
